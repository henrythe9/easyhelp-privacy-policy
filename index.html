Privacy Policy for EasyHelp

Last updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

EasyHelp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our location sharing and communication services.

Important: EasyHelp is designed for convenient location sharing with friends and family. The app includes an optional emergency alarm feature. We do not store your personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: Phone number and email address (optional, stored locally on your device)
  • Emergency Contacts: Names and phone numbers of contacts you designate for emergency alerts
  • Custom Messages: Text messages you create for location sharing
  • Saved Locations: Places you save within the app with custom names

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: Your device's GPS coordinates when you use location features
  • Device Contacts: Access to your contacts when you select recipients (only when you grant permission)
  • Camera Access: Photos you take when using the "Share Photo with Location" feature
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version for app functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Location Sharing: To send your GPS coordinates and address to selected contacts via SMS or email
  • Emergency Alerts: To send urgent location updates to your pre-configured emergency contacts
  • Saved Locations: To store and manage frequently visited places for quick access
  • App Functionality: To provide navigation, mapping, and communication features
  • Service Improvement: To analyze app crashes and improve performance (anonymous data only)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (Hive database). We do NOT transmit your data to external servers or cloud services, except as described below.

4.2 Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements and may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and usage data for ad personalization. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps: Provides mapping and geocoding services. Google Maps Privacy Policy

4.3 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only in these circumstances:

  • Your Explicit Action: When you manually send location via SMS, email, or social sharing
  • Emergency Contacts: When you activate the emergency alarm feature
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: Google AdMob and Google Maps as described in Section 4.2

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app's Settings section
  • Deletion: You can delete saved locations, messages, and contacts at any time within the app
  • Opt-Out of Ads: You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads)
  • Permissions: You can revoke location, contacts, and camera permissions in your device settings
  • Complete Data Removal: Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data

7. Children's Privacy

EasyHelp is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Location Data

Location Permission: The App requires access to your device's location services to function. Location data is:

  • Collected only when you actively use location features
  • NOT tracked continuously in the background
  • NOT stored on external servers
  • Shared only when you explicitly send location to contacts
  • You can disable location access in device settings, but core app features will not work

9. Advertising

EasyHelp displays advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Approximate location for ad targeting

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

10. Data Retention

  • Personal Data: Stored locally on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • AdMob Data: Governed by Google's data retention policies (typically 60-90 days)
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not back up your data to cloud services

11. International Users

EasyHelp is designed for global use. If you are located outside your country of residence, please note that your data is stored locally on your device. Third-party services (AdMob, Google Maps) may transfer data internationally as per their privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Your Consent

By using EasyHelp, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

17. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do NOT sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

18. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data (available in app Settings)
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your data (uninstall app or delete within Settings)
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data (currently manual export only)
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing (disable permissions or uninstall)
